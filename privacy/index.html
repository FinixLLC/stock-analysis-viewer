Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 16, 2026

Summary: Stock Analysis Viewer does not collect, store, or share any personal information. The app displays publicly available stock market data for educational purposes only.

Information Collection and Use

Stock Analysis Viewer does NOT collect any personal information from users. We do not:

  • Collect names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Track user behavior or analytics
  • Use cookies or tracking technologies
  • Store any user data on our servers
  • Require user accounts or registration

Data Sources

The app retrieves publicly available stock market data from:

  • finixllc.github.io - Our server hosting daily stock analysis data
  • Yahoo Finance - When you tap on a stock symbol, the app opens Yahoo Finance in your browser for detailed information

Network Usage

The app connects to the internet to:

  • Fetch daily stock rankings and pattern analysis data
  • Open external links to Yahoo Finance (when you tap on stock symbols)

All data transmitted is related to stock market information only. No personal data is transmitted.

Third-Party Services

When you click on a stock symbol, you are directed to Yahoo Finance, which is operated by Yahoo Inc. Their privacy policy applies when you visit their website.

Children's Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is designed for adults interested in stock market analysis.

Data Security

Since we do not collect any personal data, there is no user data to secure or protect. All stock market data displayed is publicly available information.

Disclaimer

Important: This app is for educational and informational purposes only. It does NOT constitute investment advice or a recommendation to buy or sell any securities. Past performance does not guarantee future results. Always conduct your own research and consult with a qualified financial advisor before making investment decisions.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
